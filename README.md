## ğŸ“ Key Concepts

### **Organic Search Traffic**
Traffic from Google, Bing, Yahoo when people search for your services. This is FREE traffic (no ads needed).

### **Keywords**
Words people search for. Example: "dentist near me", "teeth whitening", "dental implants". Your site is optimized for relevant keywords.

### **Ranking**
Your position in search results. Position 1-3 gets most clicks. Position 1-10 is considered "good". Your goal is top 10 for main keywords.

### **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**
% of people who click your result. Good title + description = higher CTR = more traffic.

### **Conversion**
When a visitor takes action: calls, fills form, books appointment. This is what matters for your business.
