### Files You Need to Know About:

2. **sitemap.xml** - Tells search engines where your pages are

### Key SEO Elements:
- **Title**: 50-60 characters, keyword at start, brand at end
- **Description**: 155-160 characters, includes CTA
- **H1**: One per page, describes main topic
- **Alt Text**: Describes image, includes keywords naturally
- **Links**: Internal (between your pages) and external (backlinks)
