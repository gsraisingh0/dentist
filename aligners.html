Clear Aligners

What are Clear Aligners?

Clear aligners are transparent, removable plastic trays that fit snugly over your teeth. They slowly move your teeth into the correct position — like braces, but without metal wires or brackets.

Key Features

  • Made from medical-grade clear plastic
  • Nearly invisible when worn
  • Removable (take them out while eating, brushing, or for special occasions)
  • Custom-made using 3D scanning and computer modelling

How They Work

Your orthodontist scans your teeth and creates a 3D model. A series of aligners is made; each one slightly different. Every 2–3 weeks you switch to the next set, and your teeth move little by little.

Treatment Duration

Usually 6 to 18 months, depending on the severity of misalignment.

Care Tips

  • Clean aligners daily with mild soap and water (no hot water).
  • Remove while eating or drinking anything other than water.
  • Keep them in their case when not wearing.
  • Wear 20–22 hours daily for best results.

Advantages Over Braces

  • Invisible look
  • No metal wires or discomfort
  • Easier to clean teeth
  • You can eat normally since they’re removable

Disadvantages

  • Must wear consistently — otherwise results take longer
  • Not suitable for very severe misalignments
  • Generally more expensive than traditional braces
