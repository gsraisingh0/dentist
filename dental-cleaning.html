Dental Cleaning

What is Dental Cleaning?

Dental cleaning (also called teeth cleaning or prophylaxis) is a professional procedure performed by a dentist or dental hygienist to remove plaque, tartar, and stains from your teeth. It helps keep your teeth and gums healthy and prevents oral diseases like cavities and gum disease.

Why Dental Cleaning is Important

  • Removes plaque and tartar: Brushing and flossing can’t remove all plaque, especially between teeth.
  • Prevents gum disease (gingivitis and periodontitis).
  • Prevents cavities and tooth decay.
  • Freshens breath.
  • Improves appearance (whiter, cleaner teeth).
  • Helps early detection of oral problems.

Steps Involved in Dental Cleaning

  1. Examination: The dentist checks your teeth and gums using a small mirror to look for signs of plaque, tartar, or inflammation.
  2. Scaling: Special tools are used to remove plaque and tartar from your teeth surface and below the gumline. Manual scaler or ultrasonic scaler may be used.
  3. Polishing: The dentist uses a gritty toothpaste and a high-powered electric brush to polish your teeth. This removes surface stains and makes teeth smooth.
  4. Flossing: Professional flossing is done to remove any leftover plaque or toothpaste.
  5. Rinsing: You rinse your mouth to remove any debris.
  6. Fluoride Treatment (optional): A fluoride gel or foam is applied to protect your teeth from cavities for several months.

How Long Does It Take?

Usually, it takes 30 to 60 minutes depending on how much cleaning is needed.

How Often Should You Get Dental Cleaning?

Dentists recommend every 6 months (twice a year) for most people. If you have gum disease or heavy tartar buildup, you may need it more often.

Cost of Dental Cleaning (Approximate)

India: ₹500 to ₹2,500 per session (depends on clinic and city).

USA: $75 to $200 per session (without insurance).

Possible Sensations or Side Effects

  • Mild sensitivity or gum soreness for a day or two.
  • Minor bleeding if gums are inflamed. These are temporary and go away quickly.

Benefits After Cleaning

  • Cleaner, brighter smile
  • Fresher breath
  • Stronger gums
  • Better oral health overall
